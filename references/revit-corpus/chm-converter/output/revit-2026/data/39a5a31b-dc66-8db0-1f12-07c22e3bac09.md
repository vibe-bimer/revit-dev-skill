Member name| Value| Description  
---|---|---  
Maximum| 2|  Extend the column until it is completely covered by the target, intersecting it if necessary.   
Midpoint| 1|  Extend the column until its midpoint contacts the target.   
Minimum| 0|  Minimize the column extent, so that it comes in contact with the target but does not intersect.   
Tangent| 3|  For slanted columns only. Extend the column until its driving curve contacts the target, then cut the column with a plane tangent to the target at that contact point.
