EventHandler[ProgressChangedEventArgs](11e76066-82f3-21c7-6c1f-dfbbf0a1abd9.htm)
Handlers of this event may use the [Cancel](bafd1603-f0db-4efb-e101-9fe0e3f33e85.htm) method to cancel the operation tracked by the progress bar. Users may not change the document in the handler for this event. Exception [ModificationForbiddenException](53205486-5917-7c33-8e67-e362106ddc97.htm) will be thrown if any document-modifying method is called during this event's handler.
