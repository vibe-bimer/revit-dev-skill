[Outline](1ffe9215-0dd5-358f-495d-e983f9e7d295.htm) The extents are the 3D bounding box surrounding the grid. The Z coordinates of the box are used by Revit to determine if the grid should be displayed in a corresponding view plan (if the grid is linear). The extents are not used for arc grids.
