![Public method](../icons/pubMethod.gif)| [AddCoEdge](c4713a48-712b-e293-6745-a266af97e195.htm)|  Add a co-edge associated to a previously added edge. A co-edge represents the use of an edge on one of the edge's faces. BrepBuilder allows at most two faces per edge, hence at most two co-edges per edge, and the co-edges must have opposite bCoEdgeIsReversed flags. The co-edges in a loop must be added in the order in which they occur in the loop (i.e., in their topological order).
